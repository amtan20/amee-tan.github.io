Visualizing Underrepresentation

I subscribe to Jeremy Singer-Vine's Data Is Plural newsletter and this dataset was mentioned in the 2020.09.02 edition. The data can be found here: https://github.com/taitcha/American_cabinet_appointments

This dataset piqued my interest because I firmly believe in the power of representation. Though there have been recent victories in electing more POC and women for public office, these visualizations are a sobering reminder that our country still has a long way to go.
